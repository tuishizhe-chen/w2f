03 / FRAMEWORK

Training objective

One principle.
Two levels.

The same Drifting rule supervises the complete face and every letter patch. A small CE term keeps the deformed glyphs identifiable.

One shared rule · Drifting \[ \mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{drift}}(y) = \left\lVert y-\operatorname{sg}\!\left(y+V_p(y)-V_q(y)\right) \right\rVert_2^2 \]
\[ V_p(y)= \sum_{j} \frac{\kappa(y,z_j)} {\sum_{m}\kappa(y,z_m)} \left(z_j-y\right) \] \[ V_q(y)= \sum_{k} \frac{\kappa(y,\tilde y_k)} {\sum_{m}\kappa(y,\tilde y_m)} \left(y-\tilde y_k\right) \]
